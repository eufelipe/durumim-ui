## English

👋 Hey there! Welcome to durumim UI, the most "tupiniquim" design system in the galaxy! 😎

We are an open source inspired by the Tupi-Guarani tribes of Brazil 🇧🇷 and we want to honor these indigenous peoples who are so important to our history 🙌

The name "durumim" comes from the word "curumim", but with a "d" for design, because we're modern and stylish! 😜

Our goal is to provide a set of resources that allows developers to create unique experiences for their users, always respecting Brazilian culture and diversity 🌈.

We're excited to share our components and styles with you and help take your projects to the next level 🚀. 


Let's do this together!
