## English

👋 Hey there! Welcome to durumim UI — the most "tupiniquim" design system in the galaxy, proudly made in Brazil! 😎

We are an open source project inspired by the Tupi-Guarani tribes 🇧🇷, and we want to honor these indigenous peoples who are such a vital part of our history 🙌

The name "durumim" comes from the word "curumim", but with a "d" for design — because we're all about modern design and cultural pride! 😜

Our goal is to provide a set of resources that helps developers create unique and inclusive experiences, always respecting Brazilian culture and diversity 🌈

We're excited to share our components and styles with you — and help take your projects to the next level 🚀

Let’s celebrate design, culture, and creativity together!
